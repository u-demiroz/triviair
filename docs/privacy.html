Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

TrivAir ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application TrivAir.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TrivAir:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, Apple, or email, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Game Data: Your game history, scores, and performance statistics.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support purposes.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and session duration.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the TrivAir service
  • Maintain your game history and leaderboard rankings
  • Send push notifications about game activity (with your permission)
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple's App Store
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Firebase (Google): Our backend infrastructure for authentication, database, and storage.
  • RevenueCat: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Apple: For App Store payments and sign-in with Apple.

We may also share information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and game data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@trivair.app.

5. Children's Privacy

TrivAir is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

7. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications to inform you when it's your turn to play or when a game result is available. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

8. In-App Purchases

TrivAir offers optional premium subscriptions and in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment information. All purchases are subject to Apple's terms and conditions.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@trivair.app
