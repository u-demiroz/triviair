🗑️ Delete Account

We're sorry to see you go. Please read the information below before proceeding.

What will be deleted

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. The following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, photo, email)
  • Your game history and match records
  • Your score and leaderboard ranking
  • Your friend connections
  • Any purchased items (jokers, premium)

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  • Open TrivAir
  • Go to your Profile tab
  • Scroll to the bottom
  • Tap "Hesabı Sil" (Delete Account)
  • Confirm deletion

Or you can send a deletion request to our support email below.

Request deletion by email

Send an email to support@trivair.app with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if known)

We will process your request within 30 days.
